# Roadmap

## Current foundation

- Maintain the local WPF host, authenticated localhost worker, and the
  privacy boundary documented in the specification.
- Keep the tested balanced and low-memory model profiles working on the target
  Windows 11 and RTX 3070 environment.

## Next priorities

1. Make first-run setup, model download, capability checks, and failures easy
   to understand without exposing private speech data.
2. Improve end-to-end reliability for audio capture, translation, synthesis,
   and playback routing.
3. Measure and reduce phrase-end-to-playback latency while preserving quality.
4. Expand automated Windows, worker, contract, and regression coverage.
5. Keep documentation, research records, and architectural decisions current
   as model runtimes and Windows tooling evolve.
